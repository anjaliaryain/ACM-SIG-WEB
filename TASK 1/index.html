AMAZON PRIME

Amazon Prime is a paid subscription program from Amazon which is available in various countries and gives users access to additional services otherwise unavailable or available at a premium to other Amazon customers. Users: 200 million. Amazon Prime is a paid subscription service — $119 per year or $12.99 per month — that gives Amazon shoppers a variety of perks. The biggest perk, however, is free one- or two-day shipping on most items sold by Amazon. CEO Jeff Bezos has stated that Amazon has over 150 million paid Prime members worldwide.

Although Amazon Video started out as a place to purchase and watch TV shows and movies, the industry has changed and Amazon has embraced the world of unlimited, buffet-style streaming for a single monthly fee. Take, for example, Amazon's growing library of excellent original content such as Bosch, Catastrophe, Electric Dreams, Fleabag, Good Omens, Homecoming, Hunters, Jack Ryan, Tales From The Loop, The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel, Patriot, The Boys, Undone, Upload, and UtopiaUtopia. Amazon even saved The Expanse from cancellation. Amazon is also producing a series based on the Fallout franchise, one set in the world of Lord of the Rings, and one based on the mammoth Wheel of Time fantasy series. The list of originals continues to grow, and while it's not as large as Netflix's or HBO Max's collection, many of the shows are on par, quality-wise. Prime Video's original shows also outclass Hulu's.

GENRES

Adventures

  1. Adrift(2014)
    A mix of romance and adventure, Adrift is a survival film revolving around the catastrophic Hurricane Raymond in 1983. Based on a book in 2002 authored by Tami Oldham Ashcraft, this movie will keep you glued to your seats and open up gates of conversations about the human urge and will to survive. Hollywood adventure movies always make sure to balance out the movie in terms of adventure and emotion to keep their audience immersed enough to have a wonderful watching experience.

  2. Big Game(2014)
    A gripping plot with adventurous action sequences, Big Game is the best way to end a hectic week of work. Watch a teenager in the wild get involved in a scenario where it’s about life and death, and there’s no turning back. This is one of the best adventure movies on Amazon Prime; with just the right amount of violence, the acting and the dialogue delivery. Grab your popcorn and gear up for a night of wilderness, politics, weapons, and strategies.

  3. Gemini Man(2019)
    A 2019 release, Gemini Man, has undergone a lot of work as it has stayed in the Hollywood adventure movies industry for a long time. With different crews and multiple scripts, this Will Smith starrer film is directed by Ang Lee. The plot revolves around a retired hitman who finds himself hunted by a clone of himself. A dramatic plot, an adventurous watch. Critics have said that this movie has wonderful visuals, and strong performances, but only a satisfactory plot. Give it a watch and find out for yourself!

  4. Assassin's Creed(2016)
    If you haven’t heard of this movie already, you have a whole world of video games waiting for you. Assassin’s Creed is one of the most famous fantasy video games, and this movie is based on a similar premise. The video game industry was excited to see what the Hollywood adventure movies industry had come up with. The movie takes a step towards mythology as opposed to the video game. Get immersed in the world of Assassins and escape reality today.

Romantic

  1. What If?(2013)
    You’d be hard-pressed to think of two more likable, amiable actors in the game than Daniel Radcliffe and Zoe Kazan, and their 2013 rom-com What If is every bit the easy-watching, feel-good love story you’d expect. The film takes an interesting if ultimately familiar spin on the genre starring Radcliffe as Wallace, a young man burned by a string of bad relationships who sparks up an instant friendship (and undeniable chemistry) with Chantry (Kazan) – who happens to live with her longtime boyfriend.‌ Together, they try to figure out what it means to be best friends with the person who might also be your soul mate.

  2. Life Itself(2018)
    College sweethearts Will and Abby fall in love, get married and prepare to bring their first child into the world. As their story unfolds in New York, fate links them to a group of people in Seville, Spain, including a troubled young woman, a man and his granddaughter, a wealthy landowner and a plantation manager.

  3. Two Night Stand(2020)
    A familiar setting for any fictional love to blossom. In a set-up that may feel familiar in this age of social distancing and quarantine, Two‌ Night Stand stars Analeigh Tipton and Miles Teller as two folks who meet on a dating website and wind up trapped in a small apartment together a whole lot longer than they expected when a blizzard hits the morning after their one-night stand. Teller and Tipton have electric chemistry, which keeps the film pulsing all along, even when the script veers into some deeply weird displays of boundary issues in the final act.

  4. The Big Sick(2020)
    The reason why this movie is present in the Amazon Prime romance movies list is that it calls out to the hopeless romantic in all of us. It showcases the story of Kumail and Emily and starts off on a romantic note. But, things change as Kumail is forced to choose between Emily and his family and he goes on to learn more about himself, and his love for Emily as her health deteriorates with every passing day.

Sci-Fi

  1. Inception(2010)
    Christopher Nolan's signature mindbender incorporated a myriad of ideas from some of the writer and director's most significant influences, from James Bond to Michael Mann, and is a must-see for sci-fi fans. The plot revolves around a technology that can transport subjects into the subconscious of other people, with or without their knowledge, and uses it to explore the very makeup and purpose of storytelling itself.

  2. High Life(2018)
    Director Claire Denis' English film debut was 2018's High Life, which shone a light on the darker path of humanity's exploration into space. The film stars Robert Pattinson, Juliette Binoche, Mia Goth, and more as it tells the story of a man and a child on the edges of the universe.

  3. Coherence(2013)
    A group of old friends reunites for a dinner party on the eve of a meteor shower, with the night quickly growing from awkward to terrifying when it seems that the astrological event is causing paranormal effects on the area, causing parallel universes to collide. Coherence is, unlike most movies on this list, a micro-budgeted movie that focuses mostly on drama but the high concept plot allows thought-provoking ideas like free will and choice to be explored in an impactful way.

  4. Dreed(2012)
    The second live-action movie to be adapted from the iconic 2000 AD comics starring Judge Dredd, Dredd wasn't a hit on release but steadily developed a reputation as one of the most underrated sci-fi movies of recent years. Brutal and bloody, the ultraviolent action movie sees the dystopian future cop battling against a building's worth of bad guys after getting sealed inside with his rookie partner.
